ST. CATHERINE OF ALEXANDRIA CHURCH: WEBSITE

Project Type: Website
Client: St. Catherine of Alexandria Church (Riverside, CA)
Role: Designer, Art Director



THE BRIEF

St. Catherine of Alexandria Church is a Catholic church in Riverside, California. I was the leader of the Communications Ministry there when the pastor asked us to redesign the parish's website. I led a 3 personÂ team for this project. Under that role, I supervised and made sure that the vision of the site was unified and consistent to the needs of the parish.



THE GOAL

The pastor wanted a dynamic website where people can go to it and look up information about the parish and be informed on what is happening there.



THE CHALLENGES

There were a couple of challenges. One of which was to create a website that was also mobile-responsive. Another challenge was that the ministry relies on volunteers and possibly staff who are not knowledgeable in coding, so the website must be easy to update.



THE SOLUTION

As a result, we designed a website that showed the diversity and community spirit of the parish. We also used the website-making platform Weebly because its drag-and-drop interface made updating the website easy for volunteers and staff who are not familiar with coding.
