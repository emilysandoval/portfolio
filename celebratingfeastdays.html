CELEBRATING FEAST DAYS

Project Type: Marketing and Advertising Design
Client: Marianist Mission
Role: Designer



The Marianist Mission is a nonprofit organization focused on prayer, spiritual support, Marianist ministries, and support for Marianist Brothers and Priests. To offer spiritual support to benefactors and others, I created social media posts to commemorate Catholic feast days important to the Marianists.
